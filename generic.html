About Me

As a full stack software engineer, I thrive on tackling complex challenges and crafting innovative solutions from front-end to back-end. Beyond coding, my passion for sports and outdoor activities, particularly golf, fuels my creativity and drive for excellence. I value collaboration and excel in team environments, leveraging diverse perspectives to achieve shared goals. Despite the demands of my career, I remain grounded in my roots and prioritize quality time with my family, finding balance and inspiration in their love and support.

At 16, fueled by a burning ambition to become a professional golfer, I left the comfort of my family home to pursue my dream. Joining the esteemed Lee Westwood Golf School, I immersed myself in both academics and my beloved sport, earning a B-tec in sports while honing my skills on the green. Continuing my journey, I pursued higher education at the University of Sterling, where I delved deeper into golf while furthering my academic pursuits. However, the onset of the Covid pandemic brought unforeseen challenges, closing the doors to my golfing aspirations. Undeterred, I adapted, finding employment as a sales assistant and bike mechanic. Yet, amidst uncertainty, a new opportunity emerged - a career shift into software engineering. Over the past two years, I've dedicated myself to mastering this new craft, relentlessly improving and evolving. With unwavering determination and a passion for growth, I've embraced this unexpected path, ready to make my mark in the world of technology.
